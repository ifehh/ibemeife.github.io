HOW TO APPLY

1. Buy Form

The application form will be procured if the parent has decided to enroll his /her ward at BEAUTIFUL BEGINNINGS School. And you can also complete the registration form.

2. Fill Form

The form should be filled and returned with: 4 recent passport photographs, A copy of the child's birth certificate, Last school report, and transfer papers (where applicable). At this point, a date is set for the entrance exam.

3. Entrance Examination/ Evaluation

Entrance exam and evaluation. This is for applicants seeking admission into the Primary classes. Children seeking admission into Nursery 1 and 2 classes will undergo a mini informal assessment to ascertain their readiness for learning.

4. Parents Interview

Parents' Interview. Meeting of the school's senior staff with the parents. Here the parents are informed of the applicant's performance on the examination and the proper class placement. Payment of Joining and Development fees. After a successful interview, the applicant is offered admission in the school. The parents are then required to register the child by paying the Joining and Development fees. At registration the applicant receives the registration packet which includes: Enrollment Agreement form, Medical form, Parent commitment form, Child's ID CARD/ PICK UP PERMIT Parent Handbook.
